Jinnto strives to be with a kid at every step

Childhood is a time to be brave and curious. As parents ourselves, sometimes we really want to have some free time. When our children are left to themselves, we want them to spend some quality time with something exciting and educational.

We create exciting, reliable, and high-quality toys

Our desire led us to Jinnto. Jinnto made us true enthusiasts of our business: prompt, responsible, attentive to details. Because, you know, we have the most demanding clients in the world — children.

Our goal is to be more than a toy company

Inspired by our families and the world around us. Jinnto is a source of knowledge, inspiration, and fun for a child. Moreover, it’s a springboard for a child's self-determination and personal growth.

Enjoy lovely moments

Make the most of playtime with Jinnto!
Using a Jinnto box consists of two stages.

Stage 1. The child creates the toy

Here they learn, use logic, spatial and creative thinking, exercise basic design skills.

Stage 2. The child plays with the toy

It's their creation, so it's even more valuable for them.

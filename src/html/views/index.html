What’s Jinnto?

Jinnto – is a STEM construction kit, which sparks a child's interest in studying mechanics, physics, mathematics and other sciences. And all of that happens during a game! A child assembles a kit into an educational toy and enjoys playing and learning at once.

Stage 1: Meet the Jinnto

Every box contains variety of exciting details and comics that will help your child create a toy. Once kids start playing, they set their six superpowers free.

Comics provide detailed and clear instructions to any invention in the box. Following that, your kid will dive into the fascinating process of inventing! Isn’t it cool?
